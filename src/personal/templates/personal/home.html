A Best College In Bengaluru, Karnataka

"Always Aiming High"

Established in 1973, Seshadripuram College quickly attained a prominent position among the limited number of undergraduate colleges in Bangalore at that time. In these 50 years, the number of degree colleges in Bangalore has shown astronomical growth but Seshadripuram College continues to maintain its reputation and position. From a meagre strength of thirty students at inception, the student strength has grown substantially to more than 3000 with a commensurate expansion of the faculty. The Institution is first among the affiliated College under Bangalore University to be granted PG courses and to offer the BBA course. It is currently affiliated to Bengaluru City University after the University trifurcation process.

The institution has been awarded the 'A' grade in the 2nd and 3rd cycles of NAAC accreditation and regularly featured among the top Commerce colleges not only in Bangalore but of the country in rankings announced by leading national newspapers. Its students regularly find the top position among rank holders in the university and its placement records are outstanding.

This reputation is sustained through more than 5 decades due to the institutionâ€™s ability to adapt itself to the changing needs of the times without losing touch with its core values. The college attempts to create a stimulating teaching learning environment which equips students with the knowledge and skills required to face the challenges of the globalised environment along with instilling a deep sense of social responsibility and self-discipline in its students. The Institution also strives to cultivate a warm, cordial and mutually supportive relationship with all its stake holders and aims to establish inclusive learning environment to all its students.
